Mission Statement

The vision of BRS is to cultivate global citizens who have both an international perspective and a good understanding of Chinese culture. Its mission is to gather and utilize international resources to give both the students and the staff the opportunity to explore global education, especially to experience cultural diversity, thereby bringing the world closer to families, communities and districts. It is believed and expected that empowered, studentsand staff at BRS will have the opportunity and capacity to contribute in global affairs either now or in the future.
